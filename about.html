About Mahube Primary School

Welcome to Mahube Primary School, Mamelodi's leading Educational Institution, an integral pillar of the educational landscape in the vibrant community of Mamelodi. Mahube Valley Primary School is a bustling hub of intellectual growth and development for 548 number of learners. These bright minds are guided, mentored, and nurtured by our dedicated faculty of 19 teachers. Mahube Valley Primary School is proud to operate under the aegis of the Tshwane South Education District, which serves as a beacon of educational excellence in the Gauteng region. Mahube Valley Primary School is committed to fostering an environment conducive to learning, growth, and advancement for all our students.

Subjects Offered at Our School are :

1. Home Language: This is the language spoken most often in the learner’s home. The aim is to foster effective communication and cognitive development.
2. First Additional Language: This subject is designed to enhance multilingualism, and it’s usually a language other than the learner’s home language.
3. Mathematics: Mathematics is a core subject that focuses on problem-solving, logic, and numerical competence.
4. Life Skills: This subject is a combination of Personal and Social Well-being, Creative Arts, and Physical Education. It’s designed to equip learners with the necessary skills to respond to challenges in their everyday lives.
5. Natural Sciences and Technology: This combined subject helps learners understand and apply scientific and technological concepts. It encourages exploration, investigation, and problem-solving.
6. Social Sciences (Grades 4-7): This subject, introduced from Grade 4, combines History and Geography. It helps learners understand their social and environmental contexts.
